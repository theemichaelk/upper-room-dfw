Small Groups in North Texas: How to Find Community Beyond Sunday

Many DFW transplants say finding community took longer than finding a worship service. Small groups bridge that gap.

Types of groups to look for

  • Neighborhood home groups
  • Young adult or young family cohorts
  • Men's and women's studies
  • Serve teams tied to outreach

Ask any listing contact about open groups — or message Upper Room for recommendations by zip code.
