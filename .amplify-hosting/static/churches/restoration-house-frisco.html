Restoration House Frisco

Support and housing assistance for women and children escaping domestic violence. Faith-based trauma recovery.

Restoration House provides safe housing, trauma-informed counseling, and long-term discipleship for women and children fleeing domestic abuse. We partner with local churches to provide mentors and community.

By appointment + weekly groups. Confidential services.
