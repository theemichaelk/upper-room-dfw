Arlington (Downtown), TX Church

First Baptist Arlington

Historic downtown Arlington church with both traditional and contemporary services and a heart for the city.

First Baptist Arlington has been serving the city since 1873. Today we offer two distinct worship styles under one roof, excellent children's and student ministries, and dozens of ways to serve Arlington through local mission partnerships.

300 W Abram St, Arlington, TX 76010 • Sundays 9:15am & 11:00am (Traditional & Contemporary)
