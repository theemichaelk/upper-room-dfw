DFW Faith Fest

The largest multi-church worship and community festival in North Texas. Free concerts, food trucks, kids zone, and outreach.

About the Festival

DFW Faith Fest is an annual three-day celebration that brings together over 80 churches for worship, community service, and family fun. Features national worship artists, a massive kids zone, food trucks, and hundreds of volunteer opportunities. Free admission for all.
