About Upper Room DFW

A community-powered directory connecting people to faith communities across the Dallas–Fort Worth metroplex.

Our Mission

Upper Room DFW exists to make it dramatically easier for individuals and families to find a spiritual home in one of the fastest-growing regions in America. We believe everyone deserves a place to belong, grow, and serve.

What We Do

  • Maintain the most accurate and comprehensive listing of churches and ministries in DFW
  • Provide powerful filters so you can quickly find churches that match your values, location, and life stage
  • Highlight events, service opportunities, and resources from trusted local partners
  • Help smaller churches and ministries get discovered by the community

Our Values

Community
Connection transforms lives. We exist to bring people together.
Inclusivity
Every background and journey is welcome. We reflect the diversity of DFW.
Guidance
Clear, trustworthy information that respects your time and story.

How It Works

This is a fully local static website. All data is stored in your browser. Listings are community-submitted and reviewed. We are not affiliated with any single denomination or church.

Want to partner or submit your church?

Use the Contact page or the “Add Listing” button in the directory. Everything you submit stays private to your device unless you choose to share it.
