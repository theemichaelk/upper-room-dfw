Mesquite & East Dallas, TX Outreach

Hands of Compassion Outreach

Mobile food pantry, clothing, and job training. Partners with 27 local churches to serve the homeless and working poor.

Serving the Community

Hands of Compassion is a faith-based nonprofit serving the most vulnerable in East Dallas and Mesquite. We operate a mobile food pantry, provide emergency clothing, job readiness training, and connect people to partner churches for ongoing discipleship and support.

Food pantry: Tuesdays & Saturdays 9am–12pm. Volunteer opportunities available.
