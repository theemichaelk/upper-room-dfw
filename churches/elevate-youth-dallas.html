Elevate Youth Dallas

High-energy, discipleship-focused youth ministry serving 6th–12th graders across North and East Dallas.

Reaching the Next Generation

Elevate Youth exists to help the next generation encounter Jesus and discover their identity and calling. We partner with 9 DFW churches to provide weekly gatherings, summer camps, leadership development, and mission trips. Open to all students.
