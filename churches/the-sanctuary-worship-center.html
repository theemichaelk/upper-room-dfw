The Sanctuary Worship Center

Historic Dallas congregation focused on authentic worship, prayer, and serving the South Dallas community.

Our Story

The Sanctuary has been a pillar of faith in South Dallas for decades. We emphasize deep, Spirit-led worship, powerful prayer, and practical community outreach including food pantries, after-school programs, and mentorship.

Our congregation is diverse and welcoming. We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit and the importance of being the hands and feet of Jesus in our neighborhood.
