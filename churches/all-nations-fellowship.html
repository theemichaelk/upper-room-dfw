All Nations Fellowship

Intentionally multi-ethnic, multilingual church celebrating the diversity of God's kingdom in Irving and Las Colinas.

About All Nations Fellowship

All Nations Fellowship is a beautiful expression of Revelation 7 — people from every nation, tribe, and language worshiping together. Services feature worship in multiple languages, and we have thriving ministries for internationals, refugees, and cross-cultural families.
