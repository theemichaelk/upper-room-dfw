Hope & Healing Ministry Center

Christian counseling, recovery programs, and support groups for grief, addiction, and marriage restoration.

Our Mission

Hope & Healing Ministry Center provides professional, biblically-grounded support for individuals and families walking through difficult seasons. We offer licensed counseling, Celebrate Recovery groups, marriage intensives, and grief support.

We partner with local churches across DFW to provide ongoing care and community.
