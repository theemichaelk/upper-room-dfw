A practical visit plan for Frisco families

  1. Define three non-negotiables (kids ministry, language, worship style, or outreach focus).
  2. Build a shortlist of three churches using the Upper Room DFW directory filters for Frisco and nearby suburbs.
  3. Confirm times, parking, and kids check-in on the listing page before you leave home.
  4. Visit the main service once; return for a mid-week group or serve opportunity.
  5. Debrief as a household: Did greeters help? Did teaching connect? Would you invite a neighbor?
  6. If you lead a church, claim or register your profile so newcomers can find accurate information.
