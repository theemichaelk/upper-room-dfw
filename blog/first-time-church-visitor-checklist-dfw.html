First-Time Church Visitor Checklist for DFW Newcomers

Visiting a church for the first time can feel intimidating. This DFW-specific checklist removes the guesswork.

Before you go

  • Check service times on the listing page
  • Arrive 15 minutes early for parking and kids check-in
  • Bring a friend if possible

Questions to ask a greeter

  • Where do first-time guests check in?
  • Are small groups open this week?
  • How do I learn about serving locally?
